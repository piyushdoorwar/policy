AskBetter Privacy Policy

AskBetter is built with a local-first approach. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to send it to a third-party AI provider.

Last updated: February 2026
Developer collection: None
Third-party AI: Optional

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the AskBetter browser extension (the “Extension”). By installing or using AskBetter, you agree to this policy.

2. Data Collection

AskBetter does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer. The Extension does not require accounts, logins, or identity information.

3. Local Data Storage

AskBetter may store limited data locally within your browser to provide core functionality, such as:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Saved prompts, templates, and custom instructions
  • Provider configuration choices (for example, which provider you selected)

This data remains on your device. You can remove it by uninstalling the Extension or clearing extension/site storage in your browser.

4. Prompts, Page Content, and Processing

AskBetter helps you improve prompts and may interact with content you provide (for example, text you type, text you paste, or content you select on a page). Any processing occurs locally within the Extension unless you configure a third-party AI provider.

If you choose to use a third-party AI provider, the Extension may send the prompt and any related content you choose to include directly from your browser to that provider. AskBetter does not route this content through developer-controlled servers.

The developer does not access, store, or monitor your prompts, page selections, or outputs.

5. API Keys and Credentials

If AskBetter allows you to enter API keys or credentials for third-party providers, they are stored locally in your browser environment. The developer does not receive your keys.

You are responsible for managing your keys safely. If you believe a key is compromised, revoke or rotate it with the provider.

6. Third-Party Services

Third-party AI providers are optional and user-configured. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms. The developer is not responsible for third-party data practices.

7. Permissions

AskBetter may request browser permissions (such as storage, activeTab, or scripting) strictly to enable core features. The Extension is not designed for tracking and does not include advertising SDKs.

  • Storage: Save your settings and templates locally.
  • Active Tab / Scripting: Enable you to select or transform text on the page when you trigger the feature.

8. Analytics and Tracking

AskBetter does not use analytics or tracking tools and does not profile users.

9. Data Security

Data handled by AskBetter remains within the browser environment. While reasonable safeguards are used, no system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

AskBetter does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

11. AI Output Disclaimer

Outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading. Do not rely on outputs for legal, medical, financial, or other professional advice. You are responsible for verifying outputs before use.
